## Variant warning

304 has regional variants. Public sources explicitly indicate that some variants are incomplete or need clarification. For that reason, these product docs make explicit assumptions:

- Classic 4-seat mode is the default app mode.
- Six-seat mode uses a named 36-card variant with sixes as zero-point cards.
- 24-card six-player compact mode is treated as a future optional profile.
- Strict Caps and spoilt trump rules are advanced settings, not required for the first MVP.
- The app should collect feedback from experienced Sri Lankan 304 players before labeling any rule profile as “official.”

## Recommended local validation questions

Ask experienced players:

1. Do you use 160 as the minimum bid?
2. Are bids always multiples of 10, or do you allow multiples of 5 above 200?
3. Do you use the first-player low-point redeal rule?
4. Do you allow asking partner to bid?
5. Do you use eight-card bidding?
6. How strictly do you enforce Caps?
7. Do you use spoilt trump?
8. For six players, do you use 24 cards, 36 cards with sixes, or another deck?
9. How do you score shorter casual games?
10. Which terms do you use locally for leading, cutting, trump, and Caps?
